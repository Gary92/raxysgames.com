PRIVACY POLICY

When you download and launch one of our games or visit our website, we could collect informations about you. These informations may in some cases be personally identifiable. This document explains the way we collect and use these informations.

By using our site and products, you consent to this privacy policy.

WHAT INFORMATIONS DO WE COLLECT?

When using our games, you may be asked to enter some personal informations (e.g. your name or email address) or connect to social networking sites (e.g. Facebook or Twitter).

When you connect to social platforms (e.g. Google Play Games), we could use some of your profile informations (e.g. your username and friends list). This is always done after your explicit agreement when the first connection to those platforms occurs.

We collect anonymous usage statistics (i.e session length, in-game events, device type). These statistics are stored by third-party analytics services (e.g. Flurry or Google Analytics).

Note that we do not access your unique device identifier (UDID), the same affects any other personal information. These data are kept anonymous.

WHAT DO WE USE THESE INFORMATIONS FOR?

We use your personal informations to identify you in our games, so that we can give you access to specific features (e.g. in-app purchases and online leaderboards).

We use data to personalize your game experience and shown contents.

Finally, we use data to improve our games. Your feedback is really useful and appreciated, but we need to integrate it with technical data in order to offer you the best experience.

THIRD PARTY

Unless compelled by law, we don't give any kind of data or informations to a third party. We do not share any information about you with advertisers or anybody else. We may integrate with ad networks (e.g. AdMob by Google) which may also collect their own anonymous data.

The respective app stores (e.g. Apple's App Store or Google Play Store) collect anonymous sales and usage statistics. These informations are not disclosed to us and are managed regardless of us.

HOW DO WE PROTECT THE COLLECTED INFORMATIONS?

We uses certified secure servers when collecting and storing your data. All supplied sensitive informations are transmitted via Secure Socket Layer (SSL) technology.

ONLINE PRIVACY POLICY ONLY

This online privacy policy applies only to information collected through our games and website, and not to information collected offline.
