GEOMETRY JUNK IS OUT NOW ON GOOGLE PLAY STORE

That's one small step for a dev team, one giant leap for mankind... Ok, that's too emphatic, we'll try to be more realistic.

We're proud to announce that our first mobile project Geometry Junk is now available on Google Play Store. If you like arcade games with simple gameplay, we think you will enjoy it. You can find further informations on Geometry Junk in the product page, where you can also find the download link.

Are you still reading? Go to the Play Store and download it! Don't forget to tell us any suggestion/criticism: we are a young team, we need your feedback and support.
